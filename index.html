What is Exponent?


An exponent of o number, represents the mumber of times the number is multiplied to itself.


If 7 is multiplied by itself for n times, then, it is represented as: 7x7x7x7x...n times = 7n


The above expression, 7n, is said as 7 raised to the power n.
